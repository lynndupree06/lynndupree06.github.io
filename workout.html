What is an "Add-On"?

A high-intensity finisher performed in addition to your regular workout. These drills sharpen the specific skills needed for the finale.

Fitness Glossary

AMRAP: "As Many Rounds As Possible."

EMOM: "Every Minute On the Minute."

Weekly Add-On Drills

Monday: The Push/Squat Ladder

8-Minute AMRAP: 5 Hand-Release Pushups + 10 Goblet Squats. Increase reps by 2 each round.

Wednesday: Burpee Sprints

4 Rounds: 10-15 Burpees as fast as possible. Rest exactly 60 seconds between rounds.

Friday: The Engine Room

10-Minute EMOM: Odd Minutes: 15 KB Swings. Even Minutes: 40 Jump Ropes.

Weekend Challenge: Active Recovery

30-Minute steady state run or brisk walk. Clear the mind, prep the body.
